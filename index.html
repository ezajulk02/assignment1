Frequently Asked Question

Everything you need to know about DEVCONF2026

Will the sessions be recorded

Yes! All technical talks and keynote sessions will be recorded. Standard ticket holders get 30 days of access, while Pro and Team ticket holders get lifetime access.

Can i get a refund if my plans change

Full refunds are available up to 14 days before the event (August 1, 2026). After that, you can transfer your ticket to a colleague at any time for free.

Is there a discount for groups or teams?

Yes! Our "Team" package offers a heavily discounted rate for up to 10 people, which includes exclusive perks like dedicated lounge access and private workshop bookings.

Can I get a letter to convince my manager?

Absolutely. Reach out to our support team and we will send you a pre-written expense template outlining the ROI of attending the conference to help get your ticket approved.
